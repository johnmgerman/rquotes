Random Quotes

About

The Random Quotes App is a very simple application, obviously. It randomly displays quotes along with title, actor, character name and the year. It will also display an embedded youtube clip if one is associated to the given quote.

I think most can, in some way or another, agree that movies helped take us away from life at times. Movies can take us away from whatever life brings to immerse ourselves into a different world. A different story. A different character. Sometimes, without even knowing they do so. So get lost again, even if only briefly.

Disclaimer

All youtube videos are coming from the public facing website www.Youtube.com; These are brief clips that contain the quotes, and rquotes.tk will only link to videos that are a brief segment of a full-length source, and never the actual full-length source. All of the links are from users who opted to have their videos be sharable. http://rquotes.tk is not connected to the content within the video other than sharing a link back to the public video posted by various users on www.Youtube.com.

All of the quotes within this application are not owned by rquotes.tk; These are quotes from books, comics, movies, stage plays, etc., and are only displayed for entertainment. All of the quotes are from published works, in example, movies, T.V shows, etc. Rquotes.tk would never, and will never publish any type of quote that hasn't been fully published within the entire written work.
